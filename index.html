MeetingGlass is a video meeting application that uses virtual frosted glass technology to ensure mutual visibility only, similar to real frosted glass. Key features include: mandatory reciprocal privacy (you only see others when your camera is on), frosted appearance by default, click-to-unfrost with confirmation, low CPU and bandwidth usage, no meeting time limits, no registration required, and 60-second setup. Designed for remote work teams, study groups, and social meetings where comfortable presence without surveillance is desired. Primary use cases: remote work meetings without fatigue, study sessions with classmates, casual meetings with friends and family. Technical advantages: hardware acceleration, adjustable audio per person, screen sharing, and the ability to auto-join meetings on system startup. Privacy model: 1) Visibility requires mutual camera activation, 2) Frosted appearance by default, 3) Click to unfrost individuals, 4) Confirm before unfrosting yourself, 5) Adjustable privacy levels per meeting.
